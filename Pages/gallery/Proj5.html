Phrase

Phrase is a website that helps companies to translate their website or service. I learned to use Phrase so I could improve the Dutch translation of Seppo.

I first went through all of Seppo (so I pressed all the buttons and options that I could) and wrote the bad translations in an excel document. Then I went through it all with my internship coach to come up with improved translations. It is really cool to see that the current Dutch version of Seppo uses my translations.
